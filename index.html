Walkaround Checks - Step by Step Guide

Daily walkaround checks are essential for vehicle safety and compliance. Follow these steps:

  • Check lights and indicators
  • Inspect mirrors and glass for damage
  • Examine tyres and wheel security
  • Ensure brakes are functioning correctly
  • Check fluid levels: oil, coolant, and screen wash
  • Confirm load security
  • Look for any visible leaks
  • Test horn and warning devices
  • Ensure tachograph is operational
  • Confirm seatbelts and safety equipment are intact

For full guidance, visit the official UK government guide: Walkaround Checks Guide.

Defect Reporting using Haul Tech Live

All defects must be reported immediately using the Haul Tech Live Defect App. Follow these steps:

  • Open the Haul Tech Live Defect App on your device
  • Select the vehicle and enter details
  • Describe the defect with as much detail as possible
  • Attach photos if necessary
  • Submit the report
  • Monitor for updates and further actions required

Defects must be reported daily and upon notification of any further defects that arise throughout the day.
